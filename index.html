Crystal Collector


How to Play:

  • Click on any one of the four crystals to generate a number
  • The numbers will be added together, and any crystal may be selected multiple times
  • The goal is to match the Goal Number without going over
  • If the player reaches the Goal Number without going over, the player wins
  • To reset all values, click "New Game"
  • Going over the Goal Number will result in "Game Over" - Good Luck!
